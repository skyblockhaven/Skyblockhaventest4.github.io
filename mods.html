🔧 SkyBlockHaven Mods

Information about allowed and forbidden Minecraft modifications.

✅ Allowed Mods

  • Performance mods
  • FPS improvement mods
  • Approved minimap mods
  • Quality-of-life mods
  • Cosmetic mods

❌ Forbidden Mods

  • Hacked clients
  • X-Ray mods
  • Combat advantage mods
  • Auto-clickers
  • Duplication exploit mods
  • Movement cheats

📌 Mod Rules

  • Only use approved modifications.
  • Ask staff if unsure about a mod.
  • Players are responsible for their mods.
  • Using prohibited mods may result in punishment.
