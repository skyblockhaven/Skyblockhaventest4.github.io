📜 Server Rules

Please follow these rules to keep SkyBlockHaven fun and fair for everyone.

🤝 Respect Others

No harassment, hate speech, excessive toxicity, or personal attacks.

🚫 No Cheating

Hacked clients, X-ray, macros, exploits, or unauthorized modifications are not allowed.

💬 Chat Rules

No spam, advertising other servers, or inappropriate language.

⚖️ Fair Play

Report bugs instead of exploiting them. Staff decisions should be respected.

🛡️ Punishments

Breaking the rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity.
