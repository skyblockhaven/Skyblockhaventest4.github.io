📜 SkyBlockHaven Rules

⚔️ SkyBlockHaven Anarchy Rules

SkyBlockHaven Anarchy is a chaotic survival experience where players create their own stories, alliances, wars, and empires.

1. No Hacking or Cheating

Hacked clients, X-Ray, combat hacks, movement hacks, exploits, macros, and unfair advantages are prohibited.

2. Griefing is Allowed

Players may destroy, modify, grief, or raid builds. Protect your valuables.

3. Stealing is Allowed

Items stored in containers may be taken by other players.

4. PvP is Allowed

Player combat is allowed unless restricted during events.

5. Raiding is Allowed

Raiding bases and taking resources is part of the anarchy experience.

6. No Advertising

Advertising other servers, websites, or communities without approval is prohibited.

7. Respect Staff

Staff decisions are final. Use support tickets for disagreements.

8. No Server Abuse

Lag machines, crashes, exploits, and performance abuse are prohibited.

9. Keep Chat Appropriate

No hate speech, threats, spam, harassment, or malicious links.

💰 Real-Life Trading & Safety

  • No real-life scamming
  • No unauthorized real-money trading
  • No doxxing
  • No threats
  • Protect personal information

💬 Discord Rules

  • Must be 13+ to use Discord
  • Follow server rules
  • Respect voice channels
  • Use tickets properly
  • Respect ban appeals
  • Follow staff instructions

⚖️ Punishments

  • Verbal Warning
  • Temporary Mute
  • Temporary Ban
  • Permanent Ban
  • Item Removal
  • Economy Reset
  • Rollback
  • Additional punishments at staff discretion

Staff may take action against behavior that negatively affects the community, even if it is not specifically listed.

📌 Final Notes

These rules exist to keep SkyBlockHaven safe, fair, and enjoyable. By playing on the server, you agree to follow all rules and staff instructions.

Trust No One. Build Wisely. Survive the Chaos.
